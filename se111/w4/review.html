Review

Zayd Alsasa February 4th 2021

Monsters Inc. is probably my favorite movie in my opinion because it has a lot of elements I enjoy in media. The movie has jokes, good animation(for it's time), a story line, and a good soundtrack. I saw this movie for the first time when I rented a VHS from the library.

Mike Wazowski is probably one of my favorite animated characters. His personality and funny remarks give his character a lot of progression within the story.

  • The scoreboard at the scaring contest consists of names of the Pixar Staff
